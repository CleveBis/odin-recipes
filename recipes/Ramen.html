Japanese Ramen

  • 4 servings of fresh Ramen noodles
  • 4 cups chicken or beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons mirin
  • 2 tablespoons sake
  • 1 tablespoon miso paste
  • 1 inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 4 ounces sliced pork belly or chicken breast
  • 4 eggs, soft boiled
  • 2 cups baby spinach
  • 4 sheets of nori seaweed
  • Sesame oil, for garnish
  • Chili oil, for garnish (optional)

    • Steps

    1. Cook the Ramen noodles according to the package instructions and set aside.
    2. In a large pot, bring the broth, soy sauce, mirin, sake, miso paste, garlic, and ginger to a boil over high heat. Reduce the heat to low and let simmer for 10 minutes.
    3. In a separate pan, cook the sliced pork or chicken until browned. Set aside.
    4. In a bowl, divide the Ramen noodles among four serving bowls. Ladle the broth over the noodles.
    5. Top each bowl with sliced pork or chicken, a soft-boiled egg, a handful of baby spinach, a sheet of nori seaweed, and green onions.
    6. Drizzle sesame oil and chili oil over each bowl, if desired.
